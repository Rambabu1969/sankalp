Tulip SANKALP Panel

Vision

To create a Secured and Vibrant Society for well-being and Happiness of Resident

MANIFESTO

A. Immediate Priority


  1. Selection of Service provider through GBM as Kapston contract ends 31st March 2024
  2. Painting of corridors of G-L Blocks and deciding painting schedule for A-F Block
  3. Upkeep and renovation of Lifts in all blocks

Form Committees within a month


  1. Grievance Committee to be set up within a month as per Bye Laws
  2. Block Wise Representatives: Document Block Wise Issues & address periodically. Assess health hazards from pigeons in the interest of senior citizens and take measures
  3. Facilitation of Registering Society of 660 Residents in TMACS

Society Audit


  1. Submit audited reports for the Financial Year FY 24 and call for a GBM. Basis financial situation seek residents' suggestions for betterment of society
  2. Submit Half yearly Balance sheet to be placed before GBM

Technical Audit


  1. Regular technically audit and safety checks of gas pipelines, lifts, electrical panels, DG sets, STP plant, etc., to ensure safety and security of residents

Responsible and Transparent MC


  1. Each MC member to be made responsible for specific tasks for shared responsibility
  2. MC will review maintenance tickets on a weekly basis and will listen to grievances of residents. Publish receipts and Payment Statement periodically
  3. MC shall make their Maintenance Payments Public to promote transparency
  4. Make payments and receipts payment monthly instead of periodically

B. Community Driven Initiatives – Inclusive Governance


  1. Less Cash to Cashless AKT - With members support move towards cashless society
  2. Revenue Generation Initiatives - Invite ideas from residents and execute the same for higher revenue generation and ensure cashless payments.
  3. Review Penalties related problem - Discuss with residents and resolve penalty related issues
  4. Empowering Members - Activity based Sub Committee for managing cultural/sport or any matters of interest to residents. MC will only be a facilitating body in such activities

Ideas for Green and Smart Society


  1. Introduce Renewable Energy / Solar Energy to Reduce Electricity Bill
  2. Increase Green cover of society through professional assistance and volunteers
  3. Explore option for EV Charging Stations
  4. Digitisation of Records (Bills/Vouchers) for easing transition issues in future
